Our Mission

Throughout history, workers' rights have been ignored as companies make decisions. It is our firm belief that the employees in a business, who form the backbone of the company, should have a say in company and board decisions, allowing for transparency, fairness, and business decisions that help all members of a company. Our mission is to encourage negotiation and communication between employers and employees, and our true hope is that this will lead to greater job satisfaction for participating companies.

The Problem

As the global economy gets more and more competitive, it is clear that business profits and investors are often prioritized over a company's workers. However, unlike traditional government, these workers do not hold any decision - making power within the company. This leads to a cycle in which executive board members of a company are not able to hear the decisions, suggestments, and best interests of the workers. In the worst case scenarios, this oftentimes leads to strikes, which are harmful to both the workers, the company, and communities worldwide.

How We Can Help

By using ManagE, employers will be able to "survey" their employees on potential business decisions. For employees, after they log in, they will be able to give feedback on past decisions, wages, salaries, as well as overall working conditions. Not only does this create a streamlined system of communication between various levels of a company, but in the end, will help to maximize worker satisfaction.
